Heading and Paragraphs

  • Heading tags: <h1> - <h6>
<h1>Heading 1</h1>
<h2>Sub heading 2</h2>
<h3>Sub heading 3</h3>
  • Paragraph Tags: <p>
<p>This is my first paragraph</p>
<p>This is my second paragraph</p>
  • Sections: <div> - <span>
<div style="background: skyblue;">
      This is a div
</div>
